Version 2.4.0

New features:

  • Tap the title on the Now Playing screen to display or hide tags!
  • Long press any song on Now Playing, Last Played or Queue to copy the name to your clipboard!
  • The menu on the top-right has an option to force-close the app.
  • This changelog now shows as a popup on first start. Thanks for reading it! I hope you find it useful.

Fixes:

  • Last Played and Queue views should update with less fails now.
  • Requested songs in the Last Played list have their time correctly coloured now.
  • The keyboard is correctly dismissed when leaving the Requests or Favourites views.
  • The thread message is now showing even if it's not a ling (e.g. random text or image)

Internal changes:

  • Update to Kotlin 2.1.0
  • Many libraries updated
  • Switch from ExoPlayer to media3: as a result, the minimum Android version supported has been raised to Android 5.0.
Thanks for listening!
